## About NusaFerment

NusaFerment is a digital atlas of Indonesian fermented food heritage. The app helps users explore fermented foods through curated food records, cultural narratives, map-based discovery, gastronomy routes, and a personal exploration passport.

The application is designed for students, lecturers, researchers, tourism communities, cultural travelers, and gastronomy enthusiasts.

## Data and Privacy Notes

NusaFerment may use account-related data such as display name, email address, user ID, language preference, theme preference, bookmarks, explored food records, and community contribution records.

Profile photos are stored locally on the user's device and are not uploaded to Firebase Storage.

Community contributions are moderated before being used as public content.
