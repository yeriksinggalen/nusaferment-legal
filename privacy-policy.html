Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2026

NusaFerment is a mobile application designed as a digital atlas of Indonesian fermented food heritage. The app provides information about fermented foods, cultural stories, maps, routes, and tourism-related exploration features.

1. Information We Collect

NusaFerment may collect limited information needed to operate app features, including:

  • Email address, if users sign in with email or Google authentication.
  • Display name or profile information, if provided by the user.
  • User activity related to bookmarks, explored foods, passport progress, and contribution status.
  • Text-based contribution data submitted by users, such as suggested food names, descriptions, places, or corrections.

2. How We Use Information

The information is used to provide app functionality, manage user accounts, save bookmarks and exploration progress, process moderated contributions, improve app reliability, and support educational and tourism-related content.

3. Data Sharing

NusaFerment does not sell user data. User data is not shared with third parties for advertising purposes. Some data may be processed through trusted service providers such as Firebase services used for authentication and database functionality.

4. User Contributions

Users may submit suggestions related to fermented foods or locations. Submitted contributions are reviewed before being displayed publicly. NusaFerment does not allow unmoderated public posting in the MVP version.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable technical measures to protect user data. Data transmitted between the app and backend services is encrypted in transit where supported by the service provider.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

User data is retained only as long as necessary for app functionality, legal compliance, or legitimate operational purposes. Users may request account or data deletion by contacting the developer.

7. Children’s Privacy

NusaFerment is not designed for children as its primary audience. The app is intended for general educational, cultural, gastronomy, and tourism exploration purposes.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
