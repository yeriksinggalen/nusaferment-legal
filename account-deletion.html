NusaFerment Account Deletion Request

This page explains how users can request deletion of their NusaFerment account and associated data. NusaFerment is developed by Yerik Afrianto Singgalen.

How to request account deletion

To request deletion of your NusaFerment account, please send an email to:

singgalen.yerik@gmail.com

Please include the following information:

  • Your registered email address used in NusaFerment
  • Your display name, if available
  • A clear statement that you want your NusaFerment account deleted

Data that will be deleted

After verifying your request, we will delete account-related data associated with your NusaFerment account, including:

  • User profile data, such as display name, email, user ID, language, and theme preference
  • Bookmarks
  • Explored food records
  • Badges or exploration progress
  • Community contribution records linked to your account, where applicable

Data that may be retained

Some records may be retained when required for security, abuse prevention, legal compliance, audit purposes, or preservation of approved public content. If retained, personally identifying information will be minimized where reasonably possible.

Processing time

Account deletion requests will normally be processed within 30 days after verification. We may contact you if additional information is required to confirm account ownership.

Local profile photos

NusaFerment profile photos are stored locally on the user's device and are not uploaded to Firebase Storage. To remove local profile photos, users can use the Remove photo option in the Profile screen or uninstall the app.
